INTRODUCTION

ECE Board eviewer is a non-beneficial site in a free hosting server for reviewing ECE students for the board exams, it is meant to grow with students and hopefully someday act as a source of reliable information.

MISSION

Our mission is to help prepare the ECE student for their exam by testing them using the questions compiled from different resources.
Ensuring that every question and answer are reliable and accurate.
Adapt for changes situation may require by accepting suggestion for the development of the website.
